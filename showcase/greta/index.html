    • Study of quoted vs. original Latin: Higitus figitus migitus mum, prestidigitonium! , Skypus interruptus, numquam fumes cannabis, magna cum loudly, tempura sit, non habemus carjack desolatus, e pluribus aluminum, quid pro fucking quo, ...

    • Refine the language detector: Zucchinis in bikinis, potus, benefit album, lasagna wit pesto in portofino, o cannabis o cannabis, pizza possum, trabant, quasimodo qua qua quasimodo, ...
